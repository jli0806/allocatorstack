9 questions (5 HIGH, 4 MEDIUM)

1. **HIGH: Walk us through the 2023 SEC settlement in detail.** The DDQ says "inadequate disclosure of conflicts of interest arising from the allocation of co-investment opportunities." What co-investment opportunities were at issue? Which LPs were affected? What specific disclosure was missing? What procedures were enhanced? [Source: compliance_3, p.4]

2. **HIGH: What is the current status of the LP arbitration?** A former LP filed for arbitration over fee disclosures. What fees are in dispute? What is the potential financial exposure? Why has no reserve been established? [Source: compliance_16, p.5]

8. **MEDIUM: Why did both the Head of Credit and Head of European Equities depart in 2024?** Two senior PMs leaving in the same year. Were the departures related? How has strategy capacity been affected? [Source: organization_7, p.3]
